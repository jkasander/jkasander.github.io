Mission Statement:

Our mission is to provide fresh, organic, nutrient rich produce to our neighbors of limited means. No child should be hungry or go wanting for vitamins and other essential nutrients their young bodies need to develop into healthy old ones. We won't rest until every young person who needs healthy meal has one.
